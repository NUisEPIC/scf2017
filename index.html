About

Now in its 4th year, the Northwestern Startup Career Fest connects entrepreneurial-minded students with startup companies recruiting for full time and internship roles. The Fest welcomes startups across a wide range of industries and provides a platform to connect with NU students from a variety of academic programs.

GENERAL INFORMATION

TIME: 12 - 4pm

DATE: Wednesday, April 5th

LOCATION: Norris Louis Room

DRESS: Casual

SCHEDULE

10 - 12pm: Employer Check In, Lunch, and Setup

12 - 4 pm: Startup Career Fest

WHO WILL BE THERE?

Students: Undergraduate, Masters, MBA, and PhD students will be in attendance. Students from every school will be represented.

Companies: All stages of startups are welcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I bring my resume to the Career Fest?

Yes. Please bring paper copies of your resume.

I am a graduate student. Can I still come?

Yes. We will have a wide variety of employers looking for students with a wide variety of backgrounds.

How many startups will be looking for interns vs. full-time hires?

It will most likely be a nearly even split. Some startups will be looking for both.

Will there be any particular industry concentration, for instance, high-tech, at the Fest?

No. High-tech will be well-represented, but so will other industries.

How do I know what startups will be attending?

Startups registered for the event will be added to the website. We will also provide pamphlets on the day-of the Fest listing the startups and the positions they are seeking to fill.

What is the dress code?

You are welcome to wear anything you think appropriate, but we recommend casual dress.
